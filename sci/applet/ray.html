Principle Ray Diagrams

\(f\)\(d_o\)\(d_i\)\(m\)

Mirror   Lens

  • Enter two of the four variables and leave the other two blank.
    • \(f\) = Focal Length
    • \(d_o\) = Object Position
    • \(d_i\) = Image Position
    • \(m\) = Magnification
  • Click the diagram to solve and redraw. If the page does not respond, the data entered does not have a valid solution.
  • The blue triangle is the object and the blue rays are incident rays.
  • The red triangle is the image and the red rays are reflected / refracted rays.
  • The grey lines are the virtual reflected / refracted rays.
