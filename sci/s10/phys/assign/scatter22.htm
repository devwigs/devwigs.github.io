      11. Endpoints... $$P_1 = \mathrm{(0.00\ mL, 7.04)}$$ $$P_2 = \mathrm{(35.0\ mL, 5.26)}$$ Rise and run... $$\Delta(pH) = 5.26 - 7.04 = -1.78$$ $$\Delta V = \mathrm{35.0\ mL - 0.0\ mL = 35.0\ mL}$$ Slope... $$\mathrm{Slope} = \frac{\Delta(pH)}{\Delta V} = \mathrm{\frac{-1.78}{35.0\ mL} = -0.051/mL}$$
