Lesson Review

  1. What do WHMIS and GHS stand for? What is WHMIS?
    2. Workplace Hazardous Material Information System; Global Harmonized System. A system for describing dangerous materials in the lab or workplace.
  2. What is the difference between WHMIS 1988 and WHMIS 2015?
    3. Pictographs have changed to comply with GHS.
  3. What does MSDS stand for? What does it mean?
    4. Material Safety Data Sheet. Detailed information about each chemical.
  4. List 5 key points that must be on all MSDS labels.
    5. Name & Formula, Physics Properties, Stability & Reactivity, Potential Health Effects, Handling & Storage, Disposal
