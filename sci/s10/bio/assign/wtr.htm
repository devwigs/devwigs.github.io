Review

  1. Define and explain the function of:
    1. xylem
      2. Hollow tubes formed by the cell walls of dead cells that transport water and minerals (xylem sap) from the roots to the leaves.
    2. phloem
      3. Living cells that transport glucose-containing sap from the leaves to the rest of the plant.
    3. vascular bundle
      4. A structure in the plant containing the xylem and phloem.
    4. root hair
      5. An extension from some root cells that increases the surface area, allowing more water and nutrients to be absorbed.
  2. Identify and describe the four processes that occur together to move xylem sap up the plant.
    3. Cohesion, Adhesion, Root Pressure, Transpiration
