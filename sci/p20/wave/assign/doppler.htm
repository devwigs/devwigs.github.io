Practice

  1. The siren of a fire engine emits sound waves at 1250 Hz. What frequency does a pedestrian hear if the pedestrian is walking toward the siren at 2.00 m/s and the fire truck is moving toward the pedestrian at 24.0 m/s?

    2. $$f_o = \frac{v - v_o}{v - v_s} f_s$$ $$f_o = \mathrm{\frac{343\ m/s - (-2.00\ m/s)}{343\ m/s - 24.0\ m/s}\ 1250\ Hz = 1352\ Hz}$$
  2. What frequency does a guitar string produce if a person walking away from the sound at 1.20 m/s hears the sound at 718 Hz?

    3. $$\begin{align}f_o &= \frac{v - v_o}{v - v_s} f_s \\\\f_s &= \frac{v - v_s}{v - v_o} f_o \\\\&= \mathrm{\frac{343\ m/s - 0.00\ m/s}{343\ m/s - 1.20\ m/s}\ 718\ Hz} \\\\&= \mathrm{721\ Hz}\end{align}$$
  3. A school bell produces sound waves at 920 Hz. If a student hears the sound at 912 Hz, with what speed is the student running away from the bell?
    4. $$f_o = \frac{v - v_o}{v - v_s} f_s$$ $$\frac{f_o}{f_s} (v - v_s) = v - v_o$$ $$v_o = v - \frac{f_o}{f_s} (v - v_s)$$ $$v_o = \mathrm{343 m/s - \frac{912\ Hz}{920\ Hz} (343\ m/s - 0.00\ m/s) = 2.98\ m/s}$$
  4. Two wrestlers run toward each other with speeds of 3.25 m/s. One of them yells, emitting a sound wave at 250 Hz. What frequency will the other wrestler hear when the speed of sound is 332 m/s?

    5. The diagram is the same as for #1...

    $$f_o = \frac{v - v_o}{v - v_s} f_s$$ $$f_o = \mathrm{\frac{332\ m/s - (-3.25\ m/s)}{332\ m/s - 3.25\ m/s}\ 250\ Hz = 255\ Hz}$$
  5. One student spins an electronic bell that he has attached to the end of a rope. A second student hears the sound at 1284 Hz when it is moving towards her and 1274 Hz when moving away. Determine the speed at which the bell is spinning and the actual frequency of the source.

    6. $$f_o = \frac{v - v_o}{v - v_s} f_s$$ Set \(v_o\) = 0.00 m/s, \(v_s = \pm x\)... $$f_+ = \frac{v}{v - x}\ f_s$$ $$f_- = \frac{v}{v + x}\ f_s$$ This is a system of two equations with two unknowns. We can eliminate \(f_s\) by dividing the equations... $$\frac{f_+}{f_-} = \frac{v + x}{v - x}$$ Now “cross multiply” and solve for \(x\)... $$f_+ (v - x) = (v + x) f_-$$ $$f_+ v - f_+ x = f_- v + f_- x$$ $$f_+ v - f_- v = f_- x + f_+ x$$ $$(f_+ - f_-) v = (f_- + f_+) x$$ $$x = \frac{f_+ - f_-}{f_- + f_+}\ v$$ $$x = \mathrm{\frac{(1284 - 1274)\ Hz}{(1274 + 1284)\ Hz}\ 343\ m/s = 1.34\ m/s}$$ Solve for \(f_s\)... $$f_+ = \frac{v}{v - x}\ f_s$$ $$f_s = \frac{v - x}{v}\ f_+$$ $$f_s = \mathrm{\frac{343\ m/s - 1.34\ m/s}{343\ m/s}\ 1284\ Hz = 1279\ Hz}$$
