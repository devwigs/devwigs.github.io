Types of energy related to an object’s motion are called mechanical forms of energy. The term “mechanical energy” means the total of all mechanical forms of energy at any instant. This includes:

  • Kinetic Energy:$$E_k = {1\over 2}mv^2$$
  • Rotational Energy: Depends on the shape and mass distribution of the object.
  • Gravitational Potential Energy:$$E_g = mgh$$
  • Elastic Potential Energy:$$E_{elas} = {1\over 2}k(\Delta L)^2$$

Energy is a scalar and is measured in joules (J).
