The Law of Energy Conservation states that energy cannot be created or destroyed:$$\Sigma E_i = \Sigma E_f$$

  • Energy can be transformed from one form to another.
  • Energy can be transferred from one place or object to another.
  • Energy gains must always balance energy losses, so that the total remains constant.

Energy bar charts are useful in illustrating energy conservation; the total of all bars at the beginning of the motion will equal the total at all other times!
