In this unit we will learn about visible light and other forms of electromagnetic radiation, such as x-rays and radio waves. Our study of “EMR” is divided into three sections:

EM Waves: Maxwell’s theory models EMR as a wave made up of perpendicular electric and magnetic fields. Light exhibits wave behaviours such as interference, diffraction, and refraction.

    Optics: The ray model of light is used to explain the formation of images by mirrors (reflection) and lenses (refraction).

      Photons: Einstein’s theory models EMR as being made up of particles called photons. Photons have no mass, but they do have both energy and momentum!
