Graphic Class Hierarchy

Below is the Graphic “class hierarchy”. Classes shown in red are “abstract” and should not be used to create graphics instances directly. Classes shown in blue are also subclasses of BaseSprite. An asterisk(*) denotes a new or experimental class that may be included in a future release and which may not be fully documented yet.
